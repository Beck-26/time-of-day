Time of Day by Beckham Kunda

What time is it?

This page uses JavaScript to determine the current hour and present a different image, custom message, and color based on each of the following times of day:

  • Morning (5am to noon)
  • Afternoon (12 noon to 6pm)
  • Evening (6pm to 11pm)
  • Night (11pm to 5am)

To do this: I am using a compound if/else block and selecting ranges using the getHours() method of the JavaScript Date object. I also use document.getElementById and document.querySelector to identify items on the page to change dynamically.
